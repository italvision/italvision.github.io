ITALVISION was founded in 2003 by Luigi Depra, who wants to harness his more than three decades of experience in the optical industry. He has worked for over 10 years for an Italian optical firm, and a further fifteen year with the same firm but in one of its subsidiaries in China. During this period of time he gained a solid and vast experience regarding all fields in the optical  industry. Italvision ltd.was created as result, a firm that initially produced semi-finished and finished goods for a variety of lines like Sover , Aliviero Martini, Laura Biagiotti,Mariella Burani, Baldinini, Malboro Classic, subsequently acquiring an increasing number of clients, not only from Italy, but also from Korea,  USA, Canada, France ecc.

ITALVISION is a firm that is gradually expanding, the state of the art designing and manufacturing assure high quality INJECTION sun and optical glasses, innovative and always attentive to the slightest changes in the current fashion trends, it places itself on the market to satisfy the needs of each its customers, focusing on deliverng value for money products. The high quality of our products not only displays ITALVISION’s core values, but also displays the total quality management culture that is integrated in all sections of the firm, demostating it’s three decades of experience and it’s aims to satifsy global markets with competitive prices.

Our production has interesting features that are not common for Chinese firms, that are however, completely valid and effective.

  • Great flexibility, provided by our highly trained and competent team, that allows us to produce series that very limited.
  • The ability to design and implement Italian taste and style, while having Chinese labor costs, hence being able to always have competitive prices.
  • High quality COLORATION, always inspired from the latest fashion makers in Italy.
  • Our molds have very competitive prices
  • An established network of suppliers in China that tailors the needs of our firm, for products such as casting, zinc-alloy, nickel-silver, aluminum, Steal MIM ecc.
  • An extend collection of “up to date models”, including: fashion women, men, unisex, Sport and optical frames.
  • Exclusive use of material such as propionate cellulose from the American firm EastMan Kodak, Swiss made TR90 Ems and paint from Italy from Caldart and other components from VIsottica and ideal, Abbrasive paste from Pai,ecc
